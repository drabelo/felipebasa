About

Precision and delicacy, rigor and elegance.

Felipe understands architecture as an exercise in synthesis: turning ideas and sensations into space. He arrived at architecture through artistic practice, where the figurative and the sensory were already central. In architecture, that gaze widens to investigate materiality, scale, light and void.

His work explores the relationship between formal elegance, sensory richness and art, understanding architecture as a support — an easel — for the art of life that will fill the intentional voids left by the architect. Along the way, he gathers references that speak to his own search: spaces that unite precision and delicacy, rigor and elegance.

01

MCentral Park

RetrofitHousing
Year2023
TypeHousing (Retrofit)
Area220 m²
Co-authorFelipe Sousa
LocationGoiânia, Brazil

The project is premised on the full renovation of the leisure and social areas of the MCentral Park residential tower in Goiânia, along with the adaptation of the façade at both the building's base and the typical floors. Reconfiguring existing rooms and circulation, and creating new spaces, brings use to underused areas, adds new programs and raises the quality of those retained.

I took part from the initial formal concepts, through more technical packages such as the proposed lighting design, to the modeling and conception of the interiors.

"The sinuous forms in plan and in the design objects bring human and natural scale to the requalification of a straight, pasteurized existing building."
Co-authorship and concept for the architecture and interiors renovation · 3D modeling (Revit, Lumion) · Coordination and review of construction and interior packages (Revit)

Casa Noor نور

ResidentialBuilt
Year2022
TypeSingle-family House
Area500 m²
Co-authorFelipe Sousa

Casa Noor is a residential project developed at the Marcos Lula Arquiteto office for a couple and one of their mothers, in the countryside of Goiás. The first formal studies begin as hand sketches finished in watercolor, serving both as an early glimpse of the project's form at the first presentation and as an open-house gift to the clients at final delivery. 03

Florata Sales Pavilion

CommercialBuilt
Year2019
TypeCommercial
Area220 m²
Co-authorFelipe Sousa
PhotosHeitor Rocha

The project uses industrialized construction modules to guarantee speed of execution and sustainability, while also serving the condominium's future occupation. The volumetric composition and materiality seek distance from the conventional "container" aesthetic, establishing a dialogue with the context of high-end country estates around Goiânia.

Treated eucalyptus logs are the main element of the architectural concept, giving the façade its identity, "warming" the metallic volumes and integrating technical solutions discreetly.

A modular, industrialized and reusable structure — in dialogue with the surrounding rural landscape.
Co-authorship and concept for architecture and interiors · 3D modeling · Construction and interior documentation (Revit, Lumion)
Render — Felipe Sousa · Revit · Lumion · Photoshop

Casa Lisa was one of the most challenging projects: the client arrived on her third attempt with architecture offices, with a limited budget and many hesitations left by unresolved past experiences with other professionals. 05

Casa FS

ResidentialIndependent
Year2025
TypeResidential
Area200 m²
AuthorFelipe Sousa

Thinking of a house for oneself, architect or not, is a continuous exercise in maturity. Constantly observing how we inhabit — and how the environment can reinforce or hinder behavior — hands us a detailed brief; and as architects we can reflect far beyond the organization of drawers.

From this came the annual essays in which I imagine the ideal house for my current life. The 2025 answer is a house where social and private life are separated by floor, and the utility area occupies the "heart of the house", with easy access to the entrance, living room, kitchen, garden and bedrooms.

"Between the body and the house: an annual exercise in self-knowledge and design."
Annual independent study — brief, hand sketch and plan documentation (Revit)
Sketch — Felipe Sousa
06

Casa Vértice

ResidentialIn development
Year2026
TypeSingle-family House
Area550 m²
Co-authorFelipe Sousa

Casa Vértice is a residential project for a functional, Apollonian family, whose concept is straight lines and the utmost refinement of their meeting points. The house sits on a sloping lot within a gated community and distributes its program across three floors, using the drop of the terrain to set a more horizontal volume into the site.

The project is under development at the office, in the phase of complementary definitions — electrical, finishes and lighting design — with my active participation in production, conception, and the coordination and review of the team's output.

"The Apollonian concept is inspired by Greek mythology, representing reason, order, clarity and harmonious beauty — the command of form and measure."After Nietzsche
Co-authorship and concept for architecture and interiors · 3D modeling · Construction documentation · Detailing (Revit)
Render — Felipe Sousa · Revit · Lumion · Photoshop

Art

Drawing is a conversation with one's own ideas.

Drawing is, for me, the best way of conversing with my ideas and feelings. Paper is the support for the technical challenges architecture brings, but also an exercise in materializing feelings in order to then work through them.

Freud says words are the essential medium for transferring the unconscious into consciousness. But I believe that when words are missing, it is possible to begin with images.

With 7 years of professional experience as an architectural watercolorist, I have also maintained a personal art practice focused on the senses, the body and emotions for more than 8 years — awarded in competitions and exhibited at art fairs such as Fargo 23, Fargo 21 and Festival Vórtice.
