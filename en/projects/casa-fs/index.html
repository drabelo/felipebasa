Casa FS

ResidentialIndependent
Year2025
TypeResidential
Area200 m²
AuthorFelipe Sousa

Thinking of a house for oneself, architect or not, is a continuous exercise in maturity. Constantly observing how we inhabit — and how the environment can reinforce or hinder behavior — hands us a detailed brief; and as architects we can reflect far beyond the organization of drawers.

From this came the annual essays in which I imagine the ideal house for my current life. The 2025 answer is a house where social and private life are separated by floor, and the utility area occupies the “heart of the house”, with easy access to the entrance, living room, kitchen, garden and bedrooms.

“Between the body and the house: an annual exercise in self-knowledge and design.”
Process

From sketch to construction set.

Role in the project
Annual independent study — brief, hand sketch and plan documentation (Revit)
