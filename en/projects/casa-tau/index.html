08

Casa Tau

ResidentialIn development
Year2025
TypeSingle-family House
Area400 m²
Co-authorFelipe Sousa
Developed atEscritório Marcos Lula

Tau is the German word for dew — the droplets of water that form when the vapor in the air condenses on cooler surfaces.

The reference to German nightlife comes from a festive, welcoming and creative couple who want the house as a meeting point for friends and a shelter for their cats, artworks and experiments — arranged in a house of straight lines, yet threaded with nature.

The house is at once voyeur and exhibitionist. Inhabitants with few inhibitions, a few fetishes and a striking personality that reflects in the way they live.

In every blade of grass, a mirror of the sky.
Process

Watercolor sketches and plans.

Role in the project
Co-authorship and architectural concept · Watercolor sketches · 3D modeling in Revit · Rendering in Lumion · Texture enhancement with generative AI
Renders and watercolors — Felipe Sousa
